# RemindMee

## 1. Vision
A focused, calm task and reminder experience that makes planning feel effortless. The product emphasizes clarity, gentle guidance, and quick actions in a dark, premium UI language.

## 2. Audience
Individuals who manage daily tasks and reminders and prefer a clean, modern interface over heavy productivity tooling.
